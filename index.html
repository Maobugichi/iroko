Our process

Our investment process is simple

Step 1

Initial Chat

A thirty minute initial chat to get to know you better, and understand your company.

Step 2

Go-to-market chat

A one hour chat to understand how you intend to build your first versions of product, and validate market need. Meet other members of your team, and see a demo of early versions of your solution.

Step 3

Due Diligence

We do our homework with all the information received and decide whether or not to invest.

Step 4

Decision

We let you know whether or not we will invest in your company when we have done our due diligence, and our proposed terms and conditions.
